\subsection{Part 5}
The theoretical values of mean and variance should be
\begin{align*}
    M &= st(p-q) = 0 \\
    V &= 4s^2tpq = 1\times10^{-3} \\
\end{align*}
where $s:=\text{step size}=10^{-3}$, $t:=\text{time steps}=10^{3}$, $p:=\text{probability of moving ahead}=0.5$ and $q:=\text{probability of moving backwards}=0.5$
